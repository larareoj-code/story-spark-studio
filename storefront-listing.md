## Description

Story Spark Studio is a playful storybook workshop made for parents. Choose a story world, characters, tone, reading length, and optional lesson, then create a warm original story in minutes.

The free app includes unlimited instant mini-stories. The lifetime toolkit adds 25 AI-crafted longer stories, branching adventures, complete print layouts, narrator scripts, and a saved library in your browser.

### Included

- Bedtime, choose-your-path, and family read-aloud modes
- Age-aware writing for ages 3–5, 6–8, and 9–12
- 25 premium AI-crafted story credits
- Unlimited free instant stories
- Complete printable storybooks
- Narrator-friendly scripts
- Browser-local saved library
- Three browser activations

All story worlds are original. Story Spark Studio does not create stories with trademarked characters and is not a substitute for educational, medical, or therapeutic guidance.

## FAQ

**Is this a subscription?** No. The toolkit is a one-time `$19` purchase.

**What happens after 25 AI stories?** Free instant stories remain unlimited. Additional AI credit packs may be offered later, but are not included in this release.

**Where are stories saved?** Only in the browser where the parent chooses Save.

**Can I use famous characters?** No. The app is designed for original characters and worlds.

**Is the output guaranteed to suit every child?** Parents should review each story before sharing it.
