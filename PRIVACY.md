# Privacy and AI Disclosure

- Story Spark Studio does not require an account.
- Child names and saved story content remain in local browser storage unless the parent exports or prints them.
- Premium prompts are sent to the OpenAI API to generate the requested story.
- The application does not intentionally retain prompt or story text in its license database or analytics ledger.
- License records contain only hashed license identifiers, purchase source, browser activation identifiers, credit balance, and timestamps.
- Parents should avoid entering full names, addresses, contact details, medical information, or other sensitive personal data.
- AI-generated output may contain mistakes. A parent or guardian must review every story before sharing it with a child.
