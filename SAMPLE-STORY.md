# The Lantern in the Moonlit Forest

## Once Upon Tonight

As evening settled, a young storyteller noticed silver leaves shimmering beyond the window. Nothing needed to be rushed; the best discoveries waited for careful footsteps.

## A Curious Clue

At the edge of the path, the storyteller met a friendly lantern moth. Together they followed three soft clues toward the quiet hill.

## The Bright Way Home

The final clue could only be solved by remembering that small kindness can light the way. The path brightened as soon as they tried.

## The End

Back at home, the storyteller tucked the adventure beside their dreams.
