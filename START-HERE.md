# Start Here

1. Open Story Spark Studio in a modern browser.
2. Choose a story mode, age range, world, characters, tone, and length.
3. Use Instant Story for unlimited free mini-stories.
4. Activate the lifetime toolkit to use AI story credits, printing, narrator view, and the saved library.
5. Review every story before reading it with a child.

Marketplace purchases include a license key. Open **Unlock toolkit**, choose **Bought on Gumroad or Payhip?**, and enter that key. Each license supports three browsers.

Privacy: names and saved stories stay in the browser. Story prompts are processed only to create the requested story and are not stored by Story Spark Studio.
